COMPUTER COURSES

ASRA was founded by Vandna Mishra in 1992 with a heartfelt mission to support and uplift people with disabilities. Her journey began at the Indian Socio Institute, where she was deeply inspired by her professor, Prof. Andre Banttree. During her research and book reviews, she became increasingly aware of the challenges faced by disabled individuals.

ASRA, which means "shelter" in Hindi, resonates deeply with strong emotional connotation, evoking feelings of care, protection, and safety, helping to build trust and rapport with the community and stakeholders.

At Action for Self-Reliance and Alternatives (ASRA), we’re all about making life better for people with disabilities. Since 1992, we’ve been working hard in Delhi’s slums and resettlement areas to offer support and create opportunities for those who need it most.
