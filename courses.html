COMPUTER COURSES

A beacon of support for young girls and boys, disabled or non-disabled, coming from weaker socio-economic segments of society. Since 1996, ASRA has offered Computer courses approved by reputed government organisations such as NIELIT and NIOS. The courses offered include CCC, O-Level, web designing, desktop publishing, DCA, etc. Our courses span 3 to 12 months, covering a range of IT skills and tools essential for today's digital landscape. The structured approach of six days of classes, access to a library, regular assignments and tests, certified diplomas, professionally qualified faculty, and post-course placement guidance ensures a comprehensive learning experience.

TIMMINGS

Batch timings: 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. (Monday to Friday)
Run time: 2 hours a day

    TAILORING COURSES

    In India, tailoring is considered important due to the diligence it adds to one’s life skill set. To promote self-confidence and an eagerness to be independent, ASRA initiated the course of cutting and tailoring, especially for women who want to have their earnings. This course is approved by Government organisations like (NIOS) - Certificate in Cutting, Tailoring & Dress Making (1 Year), Certificate in Cutting & Tailoring (6 Months). Each batch can accommodate about 10 -15 students. ASRA classes are held in fully air-conditioned classrooms with each student being equipped with personal machines. On successful completion, students are awarded a Certificate or a Diploma depending on the course duration.

    TIMMINGS

    First batch: 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM
    Second batch: 1:30 PM to 3:00 PM
    Third batch: 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM

    BEAUTY CULTURE COURSES

    After launching the computer and tailoring course, ASRA started to get a lot of queries for the beauty course as well. Young women from the locality wanted to explore the option of starting their parlours. That’s when in October 2008, Beauty Culture Courses was introduced as a part of ASRA.

    A maximum of 10 students are taught per batch so that each student gets quality learning and attention. From holding the brush to the product application, the students can learn all the basics, grow as individuals, and build one's skills with the beauty department.

    TIMMINGS

    Batch timings: 9 a.m. to 11 a.m. (Monday to Friday)
    Run time: 2 hours a day

    Govt. Approved Courses (NIOS)

    Certificate in Beauty Culture and Hair Care

    MOBILE REPAIRING COURSE

    The collaboration with JAN SIKSHAK SANSTHAN for the mobile repairing centre started in 2010 when 10-15 students were passionate about learning the mobile repairing course. After seeing the rising interest in students to embrace technology, ASRA established mobile repair centres in Nangli Dairy, Pappan Kalan, and Shiv Vihar in 2011. Asra has been offering a 6-month diploma course in mobile repairing, covering hardware component repairs and software installation.

    TIMMINGS

    Batch timings: 9 a.m. to 12 noon
    Run time: 1.5 hours a day

    Govt. Approved Courses (NIOS)

    Course: Diploma course in Mobile Repairing

    Duration: 6 months

    Other Professional Courses

    • Repairing Hardware Components of Mobile
    • Software Installation
