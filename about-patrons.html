EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE

The Akshaya Patra Foundation (TAPF) is a public, charitable, secular trust, which is registered in Bengaluru. The Board of Trustees comprises missionaries of ISKCON Bengaluru, corporate professionals and entrepreneurs.

The different Akshaya Patra teams– spread across India, the USA and the
UK – play a large role in understanding the complex issues faced by children and empowering them through well-defined processes.

Board Composition

At Akshaya Patra, we firmly believe that good governance and ethics are the necessary foundations of any non-government organization. In pursuit of the same, the Board of Trustees play a primary role in maintaining good governance and functioning of The Akshaya Patra Foundation.

The journey here has not been an easy one, but we have been able to overcome most of them due to continued help and support from ASRA Foundation Switzerland (AFS). While we have worked tirelessly to reach out to needy disabled children and youth trapped in poverty, ignorance and illiteracy, AFS has worked tirelessly to make sure we have the adequate means to do so.

The ASRA family has ensured we reciprocate their trust and faith with its hard work, transparency and accountability.

Even though we have braved many storms, the real challenge begins now, towards the mainstreaming of each beneficiary and their inclusion in society at large. Making sure every individual has a fair chance to live with dignity will continue being our motto.

Smt. Vandna Mishra

Founder, President & Managing Trustee

Action for Self Reliance & Alternatives (ASRA)

And last but not least: Welcome all you special children, ASRA will take care of you and our building wil protect you.
